# VocabReader Design System

## Core Aesthetic
A premium, modern, and clean interface with glassmorphism elements, vibrant accents, and smooth transitions. The app feels lightweight and intelligent, emphasizing readability and focus.

## Colors
- **Primary:** #3B82F6 (Vibrant Blue - represents intelligence and focus)
- **Secondary:** #F59E0B (Amber - for highlights, warnings, and pending badges)
- **Background Light:** #F8FAFC (Slate 50 - off-white for a clean reading experience)
- **Background Dark:** #0F172A (Slate 900 - deep dark mode for night reading)
- **Surface:** #FFFFFF (Card background in light mode)
- **Surface Dark:** #1E293B (Card background in dark mode)
- **Text Primary:** #0F172A (Light mode) / #F8FAFC (Dark mode)
- **Text Secondary:** #64748B (Light mode) / #94A3B8 (Dark mode)

## Typography
- **Primary Font:** Inter (Modern, clean, highly readable)
- **Headings:** Outfit (Geometric, premium feel for titles)

## Shapes
- **Border Radius:** 16px (Smooth, rounded corners for cards and buttons)
- **Buttons:** Fully rounded (pill shape) for primary actions

## Appearance
- Support for both Light and Dark modes.
- Subtle drop shadows for cards to create depth.
